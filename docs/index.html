How does Snippet Work?

Snippet uses a few APIs. The most obvious is found in our misc section, where we extract the top reddit TIL post for the day. Less clear is our background images- they actually come from the Unsplash Image API. Our news is acquired from the New York Times developer API.

Our brainteasers and vocabulary words were cultivated by our developers to be engaging and educational. The same is true of our history and science facts

A user's tab is customized by the TODOs they store and the name they input for daily greetings
